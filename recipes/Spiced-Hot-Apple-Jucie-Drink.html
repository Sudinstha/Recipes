Spiced Hot Apple Jucie Drink

Description

Use freshly squeezed or bottled apple juice for this winter's night beverage. Serves 6.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups apple juice
  • one 10cm (4-inch) cinnamon stick
  • 6 whole cloves
  • ¼ teaspoon whole cardamon seeds
  • lemon slices
  • honey as sweetener, if required

Recipe Steps

  1. Bring to a boil apple juice and spices in a large, heavy-base saucepan over high heat. Cover the pan and reduce the heat to low, simmering for 20 minutes.
  2. Just before serving, strain th spices from the juice. Serve hot with slices of lemon and the optional honey
